# Felix/August

Felix/August is a long-term investment journal built for my sons.

The project is centered on documenting what I own, why I own it, and how I think about businesses connected to AI, hardware, infrastructure, and energy.

## Why This Exists

I wanted a place to write down the reasoning behind long-term investments in a way that is personal, durable, and understandable over time.

This is not meant to be a trading dashboard or a generic market commentary site. The goal is to capture the thesis behind owning businesses that help power the future of intelligence and industry.

## Core Themes

- **AI**: the software, models, and platforms reshaping how work gets done
- **Hardware**: the chips, systems, and manufacturing capacity required to make AI real
- **Energy**: the infrastructure needed to support compute, industry, and long-term economic growth

## What the Site Will Grow Into

Felix/August is intended to become a home for:

- investment notes and thesis writeups
- letters and reflections for my sons
- company research tied to AI, hardware, and energy
- portfolio snapshots and allocation thinking
- eventually, API-driven stock data for the holdings I want to track over time

## Current Direction

The near-term goal is to make the site a thoughtful journal focused on the "why" behind long-term ownership.

Over time, I want to add simple software features that connect the writing to live market data while keeping the site grounded in ideas rather than short-term price movement.

## Relationship to My Other Work

Felix/August is separate from my work in operations research, automation, and industrial AI.

That work lives under **Talk to My Machine**. Felix/August is the more personal side of the same systems-oriented mindset: understanding durable businesses, critical infrastructure, and long-term value creation.

## Status

This project is evolving. The first priority is getting the writing and structure right, then layering in portfolio tracking and external API integrations.
