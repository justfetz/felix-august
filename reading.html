The reading lens

What I read to understand what I own.

The holdings pages show what the household owns. This shelf shows why. Each book gets one honest takeaway — the sentence I would want Felix and August to keep even if they never open the book.

How this page is built

The shelf lives in books/books.csv — one row per book. Running python3 tools/build_books.py regenerates both the markdown lens (books/books.md) and the data behind this page. Edit the CSV, never the generated files.
