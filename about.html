Why this exists

To leave behind more than a brokerage statement.

I wanted a place to document the thinking behind ownership: what I bought, what I misunderstood, what changed, and what still felt true after the market moved around.

A record of reasoning

Felix/August is meant to become a durable journal of conviction, mistakes, patience, and discipline. I want the boys to be able to see not just the outcome of decisions, but the reasoning behind them.

That means writing down why a business mattered, why a position was sized a certain way, what risks were visible, and what I hoped time would do for small repeated contributions.

What this is not

  • Not day-trading commentary.
  • Not a promise that every thesis will be right.
  • Not a substitute for caution around options or risk.
  • Not a generic finance content site.

What I hope it teaches

  • Ownership can be thoughtful and patient.
  • Small weekly contributions matter when they are sustained.
  • Generational wealth is built by stewardship, not just returns.
  • Technology, infrastructure, and energy are deeply connected.

Felix/August is a personal journal about long-term ownership, family, and learning in public.
