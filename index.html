Why it matters

Not a trading dashboard. A durable record of reasoning.

Markets move every day. Conviction usually moves slower. Felix/August is designed to keep the focus on thesis, discipline, and ownership rather than noise.

Core idea

Put a modest amount to work consistently, study the businesses behind the symbols, and leave behind a readable record of how the thinking evolved.

How to send the actual tickers and contributions

The cleanest format is a simple table with ticker, shares, average cost, current value, thesis, and whether it sits in Fidelity, thinkorswim, or an index bucket. Weekly contributions can be a second table with date and amount.
